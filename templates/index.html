About the System

This accident detection system utilizes a state-of-the-art deep learning model based on EfficientNet-B3 architecture, trained on thousands of traffic video frames. The model can identify accident scenarios in various conditions including different lighting, weather, and road environments.

The system is designed for traffic monitoring systems, autonomous vehicles, and road safety analysis. By detecting accidents in real-time, it can help improve emergency response times and provide valuable data for road safety improvements.
